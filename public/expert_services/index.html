Expert Services


We offer truly independent arboricultural advice to all those who may be claiming or defending legal actions involving trees.

We work closely with local authorities, housing associations and legal professionals.

We undertake everything from a simple desktop appraisal of a case through to Civil Procedure Reporting (CPR).

We have experience of giving evidence under cross examination in the following areas:

  • Property damage (subsidence) litigation claims
  • Property damage (subsidence) TPO Compensation claims
  • Tree Fatality cases
  • Invasive Plant species

Our reports and advice have successfully led to the recovery of in excess of 1 million pounds collective outlay spread across multiple cases.

If you need an experienced arboricultural expert who fully understands the issues involved then we can help you.
