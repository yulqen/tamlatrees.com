Mortgage & Insurance Reports


If you are buying a house your lender or insurer may have requested a report on the trees.

Our report format is recognised by all the UK’s major insurers and lenders.

We identify the risk factors such as underlying soil; we then survey all the trees and identify:

  • Whether any trees are structurally unsound
  • Whether any trees pose a future risk by way of subsidence damage

In addition we identify whether there are any Conservation Areas or if the property is affected by a Tree Preservation Order.

All our reports are supported by high-quality drawings and give clear advice.

We have over 15 years’ experience and work to a 5 working day delivery to smooth the purchase process.
